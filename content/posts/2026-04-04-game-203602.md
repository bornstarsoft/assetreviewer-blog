Get ready for a wild ride as the highly anticipated urban survival game, "Good Luck," is set to debut on April 8, 2026. Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer challenges players to navigate a chaotic city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, the game promises a unique blend of humor and high-stakes gameplay that will keep you on your toes.

In "Good Luck," players must reach their meeting while battling unpredictable physics and a lack of checkpoints. The city itself seems to conspire against you, with everyday objects like banana peels and trash cans turning into deadly obstacles. This adds an exciting layer of challenge, especially in co-op mode, where teamwork and communication are essential to survive the urban jungle.

As we look forward to the release, it's clear that "Good Luck" is not just another platformer; its a chaotic experience that will test your skills and patience. Will you be ready to face the madness?
