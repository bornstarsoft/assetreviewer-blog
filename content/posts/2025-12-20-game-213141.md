In a surprising turn of events, Assassins Creed Shadows has emerged as the best-selling console game of 2025, excluding sports titles and shooters. Despite facing significant online scrutiny, this latest installment in the iconic franchise has captivated gamers and maintained a strong foothold in the sales charts. According to a recent report by industry analysts at Newzoo, Shadows has outperformed many established titles, showcasing its appeal amidst a competitive landscape dominated by sports games and popular franchises like Battlefield and Pokemon.

Ubisoft's controversial project has defied the odds, proving that even in a market filled with skepticism, a well-crafted game can resonate with players. The report highlights that while perennial favorites continue to thrive, Assassins Creed Shadows has carved out its niche, demonstrating the franchise's enduring legacy and the potential for innovation in gaming. As we look ahead, it will be interesting to see how Ubisoft builds on this success and what new directions the Assassins Creed series might take in the future.
