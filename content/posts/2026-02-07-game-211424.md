In a significant move for the gaming industry, JetSynthesys has acquired the popular merge puzzle game EverMerge, which has generated over $350 million in revenue. This acquisition marks a pivotal step in JetSynthesys' strategy to broaden its global gaming presence, particularly in high-growth regions like India. With EverMerge's established success in Western markets, JetSynthesys aims to leverage its expertise in community engagement and user acquisition to introduce the game to new audiences.

Rajan Navani, Chairman & MD of JetSynthesys, emphasized the importance of this acquisition in bridging global intellectual properties with local insights, aiming to resonate with players through unique cultural storytelling. As the company continues to invest in EverMerge, it seeks to enhance the gaming experience and expand its content, reinforcing its commitment to players worldwide. The future looks bright for JetSynthesys as it positions itself as a leading global games company, but how will it adapt EverMerge's success to diverse markets?
