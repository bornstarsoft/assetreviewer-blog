The Octopath Traveler series, developed by Square Enix, has officially surpassed 6 million units sold globally, marking a significant milestone for this beloved franchise. This impressive figure combines both physical shipments and digital downloads from the first two games, Octopath Traveler and Octopath Traveler II, as well as the recently released Octopath Traveler 0. This growth comes just a year after the series celebrated 5 million units sold, showcasing the franchise's increasing popularity among gamers.

The original Octopath Traveler achieved over 3 million sales by September 2022, while its sequel, Octopath Traveler II, reached the 1 million mark in June 2023. To celebrate this achievement, Square Enix has announced a 60% discount on the first two games, which will be available throughout the holiday season and during the Steam Winter Sale, concluding on January 5. This promotion not only highlights the series' success but also encourages new players to dive into the captivating world of Octopath Traveler.

As the franchise continues to grow, one can't help but wonder what the future holds for Octopath Traveler. Will we see more titles in the series, or perhaps even a new direction for the beloved characters? Only time will tell, but for now, fans can revel in this momentous achievement and enjoy the discounted games.
