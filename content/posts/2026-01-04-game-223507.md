The release of iRacing's NASCAR 25 Console game marks a significant milestone for fans of NASCAR gaming, offering one of the largest selections of base paint schemes in recent memory. With the inclusion of various drivers from the three NASCAR National Series and the ARCA Menards Series, players are treated to a rich tapestry of designs that celebrate the sport's heritage and diversity.

While the game does have some notable exclusions, such as Monster Energy cars and certain alcoholic beverage sponsors, it compensates with fan-favorite brands like Red Bull making a long-awaited return. This blend of nostalgia and new opportunities for customization is sure to excite both seasoned players and newcomers alike.
