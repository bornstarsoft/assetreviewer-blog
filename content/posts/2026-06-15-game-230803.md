Launching a console game in 2026 is a complex journey that goes beyond just coding and creativity; it involves navigating a rigorous certification process unique to each platform. Each console platform has its own set of requirements and approval processes, which can lead to delays and frustrations if not properly planned for. From technical requirements to compliance QA, the stakes are high, and overlooking even minor details can result in submission rejections. As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, developers must adapt their strategies and consider whether to build in-house expertise or partner with specialists to streamline the process.
