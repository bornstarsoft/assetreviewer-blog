The Game Awards 2025 was a monumental event, showcasing over 50 exciting game reveals and announcements that have left the gaming community buzzing. Among the highlights, Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 set a new record by winning the most awards in the show's history, while fans were treated to the announcement of highly anticipated titles like Star Wars: Fate of the Old Republic, directed by Casey Hudson, and Control Resonant, the sequel to the critically acclaimed Control.

In addition to these major reveals, the event also introduced new projects from renowned developers, including Bruce Straley's debut game, Coven of the Chicken Foot, and the unveiling of two new Tomb Raider games.
