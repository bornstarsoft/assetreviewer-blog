The excitement is building as beXide announces the full release of their vibrant 3D puzzle game, Fruit Mountain Party, set to launch on April 23rd. This game, which has captivated players during its early access phase, is now ready to deliver an enhanced gaming experience on PC and Nintendo Switch platforms, including the new Switch 2. With a focus on player feedback, the developers have made significant improvements to stability and gameplay, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience for all.

Fruit Mountain Party takes the beloved merging mechanics of its predecessor and transforms them into a chaotic party battle game. With up to four players competing online or locally, the game promises endless fun as players stack fruits to score points while avoiding the dreaded fruit drop. The introduction of various game modes, including Standard Race and Thief Frenzy, adds layers of excitement and strategy, making it perfect for both casual gamers and competitive players alike.

As we approach the release date, anticipation is high for how this game will evolve the Fruit Mountain franchise. Will it become a staple in party gaming? Only time will tell, but one thing is for sure: it's shaping up to be a delightful and chaotic experience for fruit lovers everywhere!
