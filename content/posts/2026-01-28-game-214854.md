The recent launch of Omi Oh My AI on Steam has captured the attention of gamers and tech enthusiasts alike, offering a unique blend of puzzles that challenge players to think like an AI. With over 300 curated puzzles, this game invites players to explore the intricate relationship between humans and artificial intelligence, all while having fun. Inspired by classic ARGs and the need to prove one's humanity, Omi Oh My AI is more than just a game; its a thought-provoking experience.

Developed by EverySecond Studio, Omi Oh My AI has been in the making for several years, supported by funding from Screenwest. The game aims to spark conversations about AI, its benefits, and its challenges, making it a timely release in todays tech landscape. As we navigate an increasingly AI-driven world, games like this remind us of the importance of understanding and questioning our relationship with technology.
