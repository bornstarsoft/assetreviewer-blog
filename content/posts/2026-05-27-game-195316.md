CD Projekt has officially revealed that a third expansion for The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, titled "Songs of the Past," is set to launch in 2027. This exciting announcement brings players back into the shoes of Geralt of Rivia, promising a brand new adventure on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC. Developed in collaboration with Fools Theory, a team of industry veterans, this expansion is highly anticipated by fans of the franchise.

Originally intended to be unveiled during a live stream, the announcement was expedited due to a leak from the RED Launcher. While fans were hoping for more details during the upcoming anniversary stream, CD Projekt has confirmed that more information will be shared in late summer 2026. This expansion is just one of several projects the company is working on, including The Witcher 4 and a remake of the original game.

As we look forward to the release of "Songs of the Past," it's clear that CD Projekt is committed to expanding the Witcher universe. What new adventures and challenges await Geralt in this upcoming chapter? Only time will tell!
